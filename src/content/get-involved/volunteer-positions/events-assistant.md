Help us make our public events a success! As an Events Assistant, you'll help set up and take down displays, assist visitors, and ensure everything runs smoothly at community events like Tree Tours.

**Responsibilities:**

* Keep participants safe
* Manage membership forms and signing of waivers
* Answer visitor questions
* Distribute brochures and materials
* Set up and take down event displays

**What You'll Gain:**

* Meet fellow nature enthusiasts
* Learn about local ecology
